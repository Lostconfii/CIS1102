Do you know about Introduction to Computing or CIS 1102? What is it?

Introduction to Computing (CIS 1102) is a foundational course that introduces students to the basic concepts of computers, programming, and information technology. This course covers topics such as computer hardware and software, programming languages, operating systems, networking, data structures, and databases. It aims to provide students with a solid understanding of computing principles and prepare them for further study in computer science or related fields.

So, in knowing about CIS 1102/ITC it is where you will learn about:

  • Computing Fundamentals: Recognize the theory underlying hardware, software, and algorithms.
  • Data and Information: Learn how data is transformed into information that can be used to make decisions.
  • Number Systems: Explore the fundamental systems that underpin digital computing, such as binary and hexadecimal.
  • Motherboards and Processors: Learn how the motherboard, CPU, and other parts of a computer interact.
  • Types of Computer Systems: Examine the different kinds of computers, ranging from pocket gadgets to supercomputers.

By mastering the material presented in CIS1102/ITC, you will be able to fully utilize the technology that envelops us!
